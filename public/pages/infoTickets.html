Info about Tickets and Reservations

Tickets

We sell tickets directly on the spot of the event, starting thirty minutes before its begin. If you have a reservation for the event, the cost of the ticket for artistic events and seminars is respectively 12€ and 6€, otherwise 16€ and 10€. In case of reservation, you will be asked the email address linked to your account to verify your identity.

Reservation

To get a reservation you need to be logged in and go on the page of the event then click on "get reservation". You can see the list of all your reservations clicking on the "My Reservations" button on the toolbar, where you can also cancel some or all anytime with no penalty. Each reservation is personal.

If you have any question, don't hesitate to contact us.
